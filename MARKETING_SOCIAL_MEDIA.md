## ✅ Best Practices

### For Both Platforms

#### 1. Consistency

- Post at least 3x per week minimum
- Maintain consistent brand voice
- Use consistent branding (colors, logo, fonts)
- Plan content 2-4 weeks ahead

#### 2. Visual Content

- Use high-quality images (1200x628px)
- Include your product screenshots
- Use videos (30-60 seconds ideal)
- Create custom graphics
- Include charts/data visualizations

#### 3. Copywriting

- **Lead with benefit**: Start with "Why should I care?"
- **Short paragraphs**: Max 2-3 lines each
- **Use emojis**: Makes content scannable
- **Include CTA**: Every post needs one
- **Numbers & data**: Specific results beat vague claims
- **Active voice**: "We help you" not "You can be helped"

#### 4. Engagement

- Respond to comments within 24 hours
- Ask questions in posts (increases comments)
- Thank people who share/comment
- Join industry conversations
- Support other SaaS/restaurant accounts

#### 5. Links & CTAs

- Every post should have ONE clear CTA
- Use UTM parameters to track clicks: `?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=march`
- Test different CTAs: "Try Free", "Learn More", "Book Demo", "Subscribe Now"
- Create specific landing pages for each campaign

## ❓ Common Questions

### Q: Should I use both Facebook AND LinkedIn?

**A:** Yes! They serve different purposes:

- **Facebook**: Reach broader audience, local businesses
- **LinkedIn**: Reach decision-makers, professional audience
- Both together = 2-3x bigger reach

### Q: How often should I post?

**A:**

- **Facebook**: 3-5 times per week
- **LinkedIn**: 2-3 times per week
- Quality over quantity

### Q: What's the best time to post?

**A:**

- **Facebook**: Thu-Fri 1-3 PM
- **LinkedIn**: Tue-Thu 7-9 AM or 5-6 PM
- Test and adjust based on your analytics

### Q: What's the ideal post length?

**A:**

- **Facebook**: 150-200 characters (more for captions/stories)
- **LinkedIn**: 1300 characters ideal
- Longer posts get more engagement if valuable

### Q: Should I sell directly on social media?

**A:** Start soft:

1. Share valuable content
2. Build trust and authority
3. Then promote offerings
4. Ratio: 70% value, 30% sales

### Q: How much should I spend on ads?

**A:**

- **Start**: $500-1000/month total
- **Scale**: Once you have 2-3% ROI
- **Rule**: Never spend more on ads than you make from them
